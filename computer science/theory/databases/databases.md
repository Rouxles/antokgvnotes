# What are Databases?

Basically, they're tables filled with fields and records (instead of columns and rows). They can be linked to one another (with a reference linking them together), and can be filled in with something like a form (or inputted individually if needed).

---

# Where can we find them?

- Login Credentials
- Criminal Records
- Libraries
- Bank Account Details
- School Records

---

# Why would they be used?

Mainly to manage big amounts of data.

---

# Databases

- Table
  - The storing of data
- Query
  - Sorting and Filtering
- Form
  - For someone to fill in data
  - They cannot see extra details (other records)
- Report
  - To format a mass (letter/email) using the details.
